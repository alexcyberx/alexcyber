Security & Legal

Legal Warning

Unauthorized access to this platform is a criminal offence.

Notice to All Visitors and Users

This platform AlexCyberX, operated by Pawan Tanwar is a private digital property. All systems, servers, databases, code, and content are protected under applicable Indian and international law. Any unauthorized access, attack, probe, or interference is strictly prohibited and will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law.

What Constitutes a Violation

The following actions are illegal and will be treated as criminal offences:

  • Unauthorized access or attempted access to any part of this platform, its servers, or databases
  • Port scanning, vulnerability scanning, or probing of any kind without prior written authorization
  • Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  • SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, or any other form of web application attack
  • Credential stuffing, brute force attacks, or unauthorized login attempts
  • Interception or tampering with data transmissions to or from this platform
  • Deployment of malware, bots, scrapers, or automated attack tools against this platform
  • Any attempt to disrupt, deface, or destroy any part of this platform

Applicable Laws

Violations are prosecutable under the following laws:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 Section 43: Unauthorized access, damage to computer systems. Civil penalty up to INR 1 crore.
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 Section 66: Computer-related offences. Imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine up to INR 5 lakh.
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 Section 66F: Cyber terrorism against critical infrastructure. Imprisonment up to life.
  • IT (Amendment) Act, 2008: Enhanced penalties for data theft, identity fraud, and system damage.
  • Indian Penal Code Section 425/426: Mischief causing damage to property including digital property.
  • Budapest Convention on Cybercrime: Applicable for international offenders via mutual legal assistance treaties.

Monitoring and Evidence Collection

All access to this platform is actively monitored and logged. The following data is recorded for every request:

  • IP address and geolocation
  • Timestamp of every request
  • Browser fingerprint and device information
  • Full request headers and payload metadata
  • Patterns indicative of scanning or attack behaviour

All logs are preserved and may be submitted as evidence to law enforcement agencies including the Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C), state cybercrime cells, and international agencies via MLAT agreements.

Civil and Criminal Action

AlexCyberX reserves the right to pursue both civil and criminal action against any individual or entity found to have violated this policy. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Filing a First Information Report (FIR) with the local cybercrime cell
  • Reporting to the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In)
  • Seeking injunctive relief and monetary damages in a court of competent jurisdiction
  • Cooperating fully with domestic and international law enforcement investigations

Jurisdiction for all legal proceedings shall be the courts of Rajasthan, India.

Responsible Disclosure

If you are a security researcher and have discovered a genuine vulnerability in this platform, we encourage responsible disclosure. Please contact us at contact@alexcyberx.com before any public disclosure. Include a detailed description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and your contact information. We will acknowledge your report within 72 hours and work to resolve the issue promptly. Researchers who follow this process in good faith will not face legal action.
