Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Last updated: June 2025  |  Effective: June 2025

Our Commitment

AlexCyberX does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial or advertising purposes. This has always been our practice and it will not change. Your data is yours.

What This Page Is For

Certain privacy laws including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 give users the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information. This page exists to honor that right and to be transparent about our data practices.

Do We Sell Your Data?

No. AlexCyberX does not sell your personal information to any third party. We do not engage in data brokerage, targeted advertising, or any form of monetization that involves sharing your personal data with outside companies.

Do We Share Your Data?

We share your data only with the following service providers strictly for the operation of the platform:

  • Supabase for secure authentication and database storage
  • Netlify for website hosting and delivery
  • Google Fonts for font rendering (no personal data is transmitted)

None of these service providers receive your personal data for their own marketing or commercial purposes. They are contractually required to use it only to provide services to AlexCyberX.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know what personal data we collect and how it is used
  • Right to Delete request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Correct request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Opt Out opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data (not applicable here as we do not sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination we will not treat you differently for exercising your privacy rights

How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of your privacy rights or to request information about your data, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@alexcyberx.com
  • Subject line: Privacy Rights Request

We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing the request.

Authorized Agent

If you are submitting a request on behalf of another person, you must provide written proof of authorization. We may also contact the individual directly to verify the request.
