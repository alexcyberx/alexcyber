FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

AlexCyberX kya hai?
AlexCyberX ek free cybersecurity education platform hai jo Pawan Tanwar ne banaya hai. Yahan Ethical Hacking aur Cyber Attacks ke structured courses Hindi mein milte hain, saath mein hands-on CTF labs bhi hain jahan real vulnerability scenarios practice kar sakte ho.
Is AlexCyberX completely free?
Yes, 100% free. Saare courses, chapters, aur CTF labs bina kisi payment ke access ho sakte hain. Platform ka goal hai ki India mein cybersecurity education kisi financial barrier ke bina milni chahiye.
Who is Pawan Tanwar (AlexCyberX)?
Pawan Tanwar, online known as AlexCyberX, is a Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) from Jhalawar, Rajasthan, India. AlexCyberX mein kya kya seekh sakte hain?
Do main courses hain. Ethical Hacking Fundamentals mein Wireshark, Nmap, Burp Suite, Kali Linux, aur Network Forensics cover hota hai. Cyber Attacks Fundamentals mein SQL Injection, XSS (Cross-Site Scripting), MITM (Man-in-the-Middle), DoS/DDoS, DNS Spoofing, aur Ransomware cover hota hai. CTF labs mein in attacks ko practically practice kar sakte ho.
What is CTF and how does it work on AlexCyberX?
CTF (Capture The Flag) ek cybersecurity practice format hai jisme challenges solve karke hidden flags dhundhe jaate hain. AlexCyberX pe labs hain jaise SQL Injection, XSS, Cookie Manipulation, Packet Forensics, robots.txt OSINT, aur Hidden Path discovery. Har lab ek real-world vulnerability scenario simulate karta hai.
Beginner ke liye suitable hai?
Haan, bilkul. AlexCyberX specifically beginners ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Courses beginner se start hote hain aur step-by-step advanced tak le jaate hain. Hindi mein content hone ki wajah se jo students English mein struggle karte hain unke liye especially helpful hai.
