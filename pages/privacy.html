If you have any questions about this policy, you may contact us at: contact@alexcyberx.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Profile information such as username and bio
  • Any messages or feedback you send us

Information collected automatically

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address (used only for security purposes)

Information we do NOT collect

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide access to course content and track your learning progress
  • To send important account notifications (password resets, security alerts)
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party under any circumstances.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database platform with industry-standard encryption. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Automatic session logout after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Regular security reviews of our codebase

5. Cookies

We use minimal, strictly necessary cookies to keep you logged in during your session. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to log in.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

We do not share your personal data with these services beyond what is strictly necessary for them to function.

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@alexcyberx.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Our platform is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
